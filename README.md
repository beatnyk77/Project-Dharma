# Project Dharma

**The board game you played as a child. The journey you were meant to take as an adult.**

---

Every civilization has a game that teaches its children how the world works. In India, it was Snakes & Ladders — a simple grid, a roll of the dice, and the quiet understanding that virtue lifts you up, and pride pulls you down.

*Project Dharma* takes that childhood memory and restores what was always there, beneath the cardboard and the counters: the great epics themselves.

Ramayana. Mahabharata. Shiva Purana. Twenty squares of fate. A hundred steps toward Moksha.

---

## What you will find here

This is not a reskin. It is a **single-file pilgrimage** — one HTML page, no install, no account, no friction between you and the path.

Roll the dice. Land on Ganesha, and rise. Land on Ravana, and learn what arrogance costs. Stand with Draupadi in her darkest hour, or turn away. The choice is yours. The karma remembers.

- **20 narrative events** drawn from the epics — ladders of grace, snakes of hubris, and crossroads where dharma is tested
- **A living karma system** that rewards righteousness and tempts you with shortcuts
- **Boons that persist** across lifetimes — Hanuman's Leap, Krishna's Counsel, True Dharma — earned, not given
- **Four sacred boards**, each unlocked through victory and ascension
- **Prestige. New Game+. Divine Sparks.** For those who have reached Moksha and wish to walk the path again, wiser and harder
- **A shareable run summary** — because every pilgrimage deserves a witness

Your progress lives in your browser. Your story belongs to you.

---

## Begin your yatra

No build step. No dependencies to chase.

1. Clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. Roll Fate

That is all.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/beatnyk77/Project-Dharma.git
cd Project-Dharma
open index.html
```

Or play directly at **[beatnyk77.github.io/Project-Dharma](https://beatnyk77.github.io/Project-Dharma/)**.

## For the technically curious

Built with HTML, Tailwind CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. One file. Local storage for persistence. Parchment aesthetic. Accessible by design.

The referral link in the share card is a placeholder — replace `CONFIG.REFERRAL_BASE` in `index.html` when you are ready to invite others to the path.

## A note on dharma

This game does not preach. It presents. You will be offered glory when righteousness is harder. You will be pulled down when pride goes unchecked. You will be lifted when devotion is genuine.

The board does not judge. It reflects.

*Svadharma — one's own duty, performed without attachment to the fruit.*

Roll the dice. Walk the path. See what remains when you reach square one hundred.
